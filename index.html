Lamb Kabab

Marinate cubed lamb leg in 1/2 cup olive oil, the juice of 1 lemon, some fresh rosemary and 3 smashed garlic cloves; add salt and pepper. Skewer with zucchini and grill.

Skirt Steak

Let 1 skirt steak (about 1 1/4 pounds) come to room temperature. Pat dry and season with salt and pepper. Grill on oiled grates over medium-high heat, 2 to 5 minutes per side, depending on the thickness, for medium rare.

Rice Kheer

Rice kheer is basically Indian version of rice pudding made with basmati rice, whole milk, sugar, nuts and spices. It is also one of the most popular kheer variant made in North Indian cuisine.
